--- title: "Robert Furchgott Medical Student Award" location: "Brooklyn, NY" ---

Andre Valentin, a fourth year medical student who will be starting general surgery at Northshore-LIJ received the Robert Furchgott Medical Student Research Award for his research in the role of REG1 and chemoresistance in Pancreatic Cancer.

This prestigious award is given to one medical student each year who demonstrates significant scientific merit. During medical school, Andre dedicated 1 year to basic science research, during which time he was mentored by Dr. Chongmin Huan. Andre also received the Scientific Forum Excellence in Research Award at the American College of Surgeons Clinical Congress in October 2015.
