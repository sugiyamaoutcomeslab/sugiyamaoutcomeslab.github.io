--- title: SUNY Downstate Annual Research Day location: "Brooklyn, NY" ---

Eleven residents, medical students and PhD students participated in the Annual Research Day hosted by The School of Graduate Studies. This yearly event showcases the extensive and groundbreaking research at Downstate, with more than 180 participants presenting both basic science and clinical research. Thank you to the School of Graduate Studies for inviting us to participate every year.
